Why I built this

mesqual — After years of rebuilding importers, analysis pipelines, and visualization scripts for every new modeling platform and study, I realized the high-level workflow is always the same. mesqual is my attempt at a unified package I can use across platforms and studies, continuously extending a shared library of modular components.
energy-repset — Identifying representative years/months/weeks/days kept coming up throughout my projects. After tackling it enough times, I wanted to formalize the approach as a unified framework. I re-read one of my favorite papers and got the inspiration from Warren B. Powell's "A Unified Framework for Stochastic Optimization", so I formulated it and wrapped it in a modular package.
captain-arro — For map visualizations in my mesqual studies I wanted customizable animated flow arrows, so I built a small package around it.
